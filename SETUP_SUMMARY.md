## 🎯 Key Design Decisions

### Why English-First?
Pakistani Gen Z experience emotional code-switching - they feel in English when being vulnerable. This app honors that linguistic reality rather than forcing Urdu/Hinglish.

### Why Minimal Design?
Mental wellness app should be calming, not overwhelming. No unnecessary features, no noise - just what helps.

### Why Anthropic Claude?
- Context-aware and culturally intelligent
- Can understand nuanced emotional situations
- Non-judgmental responses
- Can escalate safely when needed

### Why No External Analytics?
Privacy is paramount. Users should feel safe knowing their mental health data isn't tracked, sold, or analyzed for marketing.
